ABOUT US

We Leading Industry Since 1989

Why use Pneumark?

Pneumark has been established in Western Australia, servicing the compressed air industry since 1989. We have maintained an emphasis on providing reliable, leading brand products to our customers, backed up by comprehensive and accredited aftermarket services.

Our team of qualified and experienced sales and service technicians remain committed to providing all our customers, both large and small, a complete support service tailored to suit their individual needs. This includes advice on purchasing the right products, industrial air compressors, air audits, installations, upgrades, inspection of plant, 24hr breakdown service and competitively priced service and maintenance programs.

Our focus for the future is to maintain our high level of customer service, while continuing to build on our product base with innovative, smarter and more environmentally efficient technology.
